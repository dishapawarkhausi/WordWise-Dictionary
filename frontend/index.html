About WordWise

WordWise is a modern online dictionary that helps you translate, define, and pronounce words in multiple languages. Whether you're learning a new language or just curious about word meanings, WordWise gives you fast, accurate, and voice-supported results. We aim to make word learning fun, easy, and accessible to everyone around the world.

Privacy Policy

WordWise values your privacy. We do not collect or store any personal information without your permission. Your voice inputs (if used) are only processed temporarily and never stored. Search history is saved locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time. We do not share your data with third parties.
